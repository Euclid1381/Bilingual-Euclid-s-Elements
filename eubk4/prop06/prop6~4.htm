S		 Book IV   Proposition 6
I say next that it is also right-angled.

For, since
    the straight line BD is a diameter of the circle ABCD,
therefore
    BAD is a semicircle;
therefore
    the angle BAD is right. [III.31]

For the same reason
    each of the angles ABC, BCD, CDA is also right;
therefore
    the quadrilateral ABCD is right-angled.
