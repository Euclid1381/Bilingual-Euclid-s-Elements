S		 Book IV   Proposition 14
    Let the angles BCD, CDE be bisected by the straight lines CF, DF respectively,
and from the point F, at which the straight lines meet,
    let the straight lines FB, FA, FE be joined to the points B, A, E.

Then, in a manner similar to the proceding proposition, it can can be proved that
    the angles CBA, BAE, AED have also been bisected by the straight lines FB, FA, FE restectively.
