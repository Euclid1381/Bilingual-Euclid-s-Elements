And, since
    the straight line CG standing on EB makes the adjacent angles EGC, CGB equal to two right angles,
therefore
    the remaining angle CGB is also one-third of two right angles.

Therefore
    the angles EGD, DGC, CGB are equal to one another;
so that
    the angles vertical to them, the angles BGA, AGF, FGE, are equal.
