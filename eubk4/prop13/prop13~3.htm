Then, since BC is equal to CD, and CF is common,
    the two sides BC, CF are equal to the two sides DC, CF; and the angle BCF is equal to the angle DCF;
therefore
    the base BF is equal to the base DF, and the triangle BCF is equal to the triangle DCF,
