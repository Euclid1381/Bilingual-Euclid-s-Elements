S		 Book IV   Proposition 13
Therefore
    the five straight lines FG, FH, FK, FL, FM are equal to one another.

Therefore
    the circle described with center F and distance one of the straight lines FG, FH, FK, FL, FM will pass also through the remaining points;
and
    it will touch the straight lines AB, BC, CD, DE, EA, because the angles at the points G, H, K, L, M are right.
