S		 Book IV   Proposition 10
Let any straight line AB be set out, and
    let it be cut at the point C so that the rectangle contained by AB, BC is equal to the square on CA; [II.11]
with center A and distance AB
    let the circle BDE be described,
and
    let there be fitted in the circle BDE the straight line BD equal to the straight line AC which is not greater than the diameter of the circle BDE. [IV.1]

    Let AD, DC be joined,
and
    let the circle ACD be circumscribed about the triangle ACD. [IV.5]
