S		 Book III   Proposition 31
I say further that
    the angle of the greater segment, namely that contained by the circumference ABC and the straight line AC, is greater than a right angle;
and
    the angle of the less segment, namely that contained by the circumference ADC and the straight line AC, is less than a right angle.
