S		 Book III   Proposition 7
I say again that another straight line equal to FG will not fall on the circle from the point F.

For, if possible, let FK so fall.

Then, since
    FK is equal to FG and FH to FG,
    FK is also equal to FH,
the nearer to the straight line through the center being thus equal to the more remote: which is impossible.
