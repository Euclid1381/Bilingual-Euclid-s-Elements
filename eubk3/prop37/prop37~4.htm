But
    the angle DEF is right;
therefore
    the angle DEF is also right.

And FB produced is a diameter; and
    the straight line drawn at right angles to the diameter of a circle, form its extremity, touches the circle; [III.16, Por.]
therefore
    DB touches the circle.

Similarly this can be proved to be the case even if the center be on AC.

Being what it was required to prove.
