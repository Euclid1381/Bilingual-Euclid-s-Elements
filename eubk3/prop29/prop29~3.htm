S		 Book III   Proposition 29
Now, since
    the circumference BGC is equal to the circumference EHF,
    the angle BKC is also equal to the angle ELF. [III.27]

And, since the circles ABC, DEF are equal,
the radii are also equal;
therefore
    the two sides BK, KC are equal to the two sides EL, LF; and they contain equal angles;
therefore
    the base BC is equal to the base EF. [I.4]

Being what it was required to prove.
