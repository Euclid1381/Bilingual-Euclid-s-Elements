S		 Book III   Proposition 1
    Let a straight line AB be drawn through the circle at random,
and
    let it be bisected at the point D;
from D
    let DC be drawn at right angles to AB and let it be drawn through to E;
    let CE be bisected at F.

I say that F is the center of the circle ABC.
