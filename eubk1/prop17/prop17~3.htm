S		 Book I   Proposition 17
(Which is to say that the angle ABC is less than the angle ACD.)

    Let the angle BCA be added to each;
therefore
    the angles BCA, ABC are less than the angles BCA, ACD.

But
    the angles BCA, ACD are equal to two right angles. [1.13]
Therefore
    the angles BCA, ABC are less than two right angles.
